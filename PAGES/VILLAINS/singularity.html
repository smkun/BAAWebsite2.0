Singularity: The Quantum Collapse

Adrian Cross clashed with Mark Sherman from their first meeting at MIT's Quantum Research Division. While Sherman pursued quantum containment, Cross obsessed over dark matter theories. His controversial proposals about harnessing dark matter for dimensional manipulation made even senior faculty nervous.

Early Career:

  • Physics prodigy specializing in dark matter research
  • Built MIT's first dark matter detection array at age 22
  • Developed theories about dark matter manipulation
  • Competed with Sherman for funding and lab space

The Breaking Point:

  • Sherman's containment field accident created a quantum surge
  • The wave destabilized Cross's dark matter experiment
  • Dark matter flooded his lab, bonding with his cellular structure
  • The exposure compressed him to a microscopic size

The Transformation:

  • Gained the ability to generate and control dark matter
  • Learned to create dark matter constructs
  • Could tap into dark matter for energy projection
  • Body became partially composed of dark matter

Descent:

  • Sherman's accident destroyed years of research data
  • The university terminated his dangerous experiments
  • Began stealing equipment to continue work
  • Used new powers to raid research facilities

Current Activities:

  • Runs illegal dark matter experiments
  • Creates unstable dark matter fields
  • Infiltrates physics labs using reduced size
  • Seeks to prove dark matter theories
  • Plots revenge against Dr. Quantum

His obsession with dark matter research grows more dangerous. Each failed experiment risks creating gravitational anomalies that could devastate Boston. Yet he refuses to stop until he masters the fundamental forces that transformed him.
