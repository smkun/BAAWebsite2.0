Elena Thompson (Psi Witch)

Elena was the eldest of Maria Thompson's daughters, taking on responsibility from an early age. While her sisters and cousin Isaiah played in the streets of Dorchester, Elena worked in the diner's kitchen, listening to customers' thoughts without realizing her ability was unusual.

Her telepathic powers emerged at 13 when she accidentally projected a customer's violent thoughts onto nearby patrons, causing panic. Maria recognized what was happening and taught Elena meditation techniques passed down through generations to control her gift.

By 16, Elena mastered basic telepathy but struggled with ethics. She used her powers to warn her mother about dangerous customers and helped Isaiah avoid street conflicts, stepping in when his impulsiveness threatened trouble.

After graduating high school, Elena attended Boston University on scholarship, studying psychology to understand her abilities better. She worked part-time at Massachusetts General Hospital, using her powers discreetly to help trauma patients. Her research into memory pathways unlocked more advanced abilities, including memory manipulation and thought projection.

When Maria died, Elena left her promising career. Her powers evolved from healing to offense—she could now inflict psychic pain, extract information forcibly, and implant false memories. The once-compassionate healer became the Tribulation's interrogator, using psychological torture to extract information about Detective Costigan's corruption network.

Elena remains the most rational of the group, torn between vengeance and her healer's instinct. She's the strategic mind behind their operations but sometimes questions whether their path honors what her mother taught her.
