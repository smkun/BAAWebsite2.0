Danielle Thompson (Tempest)

Middle child Danielle was the family storm. From early age, her emotions strongly affected Boston's weather—her tantrums bringing thunderstorms and her joy creating perfect sunny days. Maria initially dismissed these coincidences until a school bully pushed 10-year-old Danielle too far. The resulting lightning strike that hit the school playground injured no one but left a perfect circle of scorched earth around the bully.

Unlike Elena's disciplined approach to her powers, Danielle struggled with control. Her tight bond with Isaiah helped—he understood her volatility and taught her to channel anger into focused bursts rather than wild storms.

At 14, Danielle ran away to Providence after a fight with her mother, triggering the worst blizzard Boston had seen in decades. When police found her three days later, Maria sought help from a weather scientist who confirmed Danielle's abilities and taught her atmospheric pressure control techniques.

Danielle became the cheerful counterbalance to Elena's seriousness and Isaiah's intensity. She took community college classes in meteorology while working at the diner, helping customers with her sunny disposition while using her powers to ensure good weather for outdoor events.

After Maria's death, Danielle's grief manifested as a three-day downpour that flooded parts of Dorchester. Her powers intensified with her rage—she could now generate targeted lightning strikes and create localized atmospheric pressure changes to incapacitate enemies.

Within the Tribulation, Danielle serves as the group's heavy hitter, capable of disabling electronics and creating diversions through weather manipulation. She maintains her affable personality as a front when gathering intelligence but unleashes devastating storms during operations.
