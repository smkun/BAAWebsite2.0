Voidstep: Steps Between Worlds

The Temple's Edge Todo Aoi grew up in a remote temple outside Kyoto, training in martial arts under his grandfather, a master of an ancient fighting style combining meditation and movement. The temple sat at a nexus point of mystical energies, though Todo remained unaware of its significance through his childhood.

His natural aptitude for martial arts manifested in two distinct ways - an ability to read and counter opponents' movements, and moments where he seemed to move through space in impossible ways during sparring matches.

The Sorcerer's Eye Doctor Strange first sensed Todo's potential during an astral survey of mystical nexus points. He observed the young martial artist unconsciously tapping into dimensional energies during his training, combining them with his fighting techniques.

The Ancient One's scrolls spoke of warrior-mages who could step between dimensions in combat, but the technique had been lost for centuries. Todo's natural ability to merge martial arts with dimensional manipulation marked him as a potential heir to this legacy.

The Awakening Strange arranged to visit the temple, arriving as Todo experienced his first conscious teleport during an intensive training session. Pushed to his limits, Todo instinctively merged his defensive stance with a dimensional step, vanishing from before an attack and appearing behind his sparring partner.

The temple's mystic energies surged in response, awakening Todo's latent magical abilities. His martial arts forms began incorporating short-range teleports, his body flowing through space as naturally as through stances.

The Path Between Under Strange's guidance, Todo learned to control his unique fusion of magic and martial arts. His defensive techniques evolved to include dimensional displacement, while his attacks incorporated teleportation to strike from unexpected angles.

His signature "Void Step" technique emerged from combining his blink ability with traditional counter-attacks. By reading an opponent's movement and teleporting in response, he could turn their own force against them through reverse-momentum throws.

The Academy's Halls Strange sponsored Todo's admission to BAA, recognizing he needed both mystical and superhero training. The Academy's controlled environment let him refine his abilities without risking dimensional instability.

Todo's training focuses on combining his powers in new ways: Chain strikes executed through multiple teleports, defense stances enhanced by spatial manipulation, and rapid attack sequences using blink barrage to overwhelm opponents.

Present Day Todo maintains a strict training regimen at BAA, practicing his martial arts forms in the early morning hours. His quarters feature a small meditation space where he studies mystical texts provided by Strange.

He serves as a bridge between the Academy's conventional combat instructors and mystical mentors, helping other students understand how different power types can complement each other. His ability to swap places with allies during combat makes him a valuable training partner for new recruits learning team tactics.

The other students often find him in the Academy's meditation garden, moving through forms that seem to bend space around him. His calm demeanor masks the intensity of his training, as he works to perfect the integration of martial arts and dimensional magic.

His presence at BAA represents a revival of an ancient mystical combat tradition, combining the discipline of martial arts with the power of dimensional manipulation. While still learning to master his abilities, he embodies the potential for merging physical and mystical combat techniques in new ways.

The Voidbound Triskelion - "Three segments, one path through the void"

Ancient scrolls in Todo's temple spoke of a weapon forged at dimensional crossroads - a sanjiegun split between realities by mystic smiths. Dr. Strange recovered its scattered pieces, sensing their resonance with Todo's abilities. Each section holds memories of different dimensions, letting Todo channel his teleportation powers through the weapon's ancient magic.

The +2 damage comes from the weapon striking through multiple dimensional planes simultaneously. In combat, blue energy ripples mark its path through space.
