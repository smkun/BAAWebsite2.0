The Greenhouse

Origin:
Founded in the late 1990s by a group of disillusioned environmental scientists and activists, The Greenhouse was established by those who believed that traditional conservation and protest methods were failing to address the escalating climate crisis.

Structure:
- Operates in cells, with each cell specializing in a different aspect of their mission.
- At the top is a council of seven known as "The Cultivators," each representing a different biome of Earth.
- Dr. Vivian Bloom leads the Research & Development cell, focusing on creating enhanced plant life and bioweapons.

Goals:
- To "reclaim" urban areas for nature by developing plant-based lifeforms capable of overtaking and transforming cities into self-sustaining ecosystems.
- Ultimately, to create a new balance between humanity and nature, even if it requires drastically reducing the human population.

Methods:
- Utilizes a mix of legitimate front companies, eco-terrorism, and advanced biotechnology.
- Has developed several genetically modified plant species designed to rapidly grow in urban environments, some with defensive capabilities.
- Employs hackers to target industrial control systems, causing "accidents" that damage urban infrastructure and facilitate the spread of their enhanced flora.

Boston Connection:
- Boston was chosen for its abundance of research institutions and strategic coastal location.
- Maintains several legitimate botanical research facilities around the city as fronts for radical experiments.
- Has been slowly introducing enhanced plant species into Boston's parks and green spaces, creating hidden networks of controlled flora.

Current Status:
- The escape of Sunny has thrown The Greenhouse into disarray; some members see it as a sign to reconsider their methods, while others—such as Dr. Bloom—remain determined to create controllable plant-based lifeforms.
- The organization is currently split between those who wish to capture Sunny for further study and those who view the plant being as a threat to be eliminated.
- Unbeknownst to most members, "Rootmother" has made contact with an alien plant intelligence and is planning to take The Greenhouse's mission to a cosmic scale.
