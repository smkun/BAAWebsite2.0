Tribulation

Origins and Motivation:
Formed after the murder of Maria Thompson, who ran the Blue Plate Special diner
Detective Costigan's corruption allowed the murder to happen unpunished
Four members, all related by blood, united to seek justice when the system failed them

Operational Structure:
Base of operations in abandoned Blue Plate Special diner
Highly mobile, rarely staying in one location for extended periods
Uses Elena's telepathic link for secure communications
Combines intelligence gathering with direct action

Tactics and Methods:
Intelligence gathering through Selena's shadow infiltration
Information extraction via Elena's telepathic abilities
Weather-based diversions and infrastructure disruption by Danielle
Precision strikes led by Isaiah using kinetic control
Targets corrupt officials, gang members, and infrastructure supporting them

Current Objectives:
Primary goal: Build case against Detective Costigan and his corruption network
Secondary goal: Dismantle the gang organization responsible for Maria's death
Gathering evidence to expose systemic corruption in Boston law enforcement
Collecting proof of police-criminal collaboration

Ethical Position:
Not traditional heroes or villains
Operate outside the law but with self-imposed limits
Avoid civilian casualties but willing to use excessive force against targets
Primarily focused on justice rather than random destruction

Known Activities:
Targeted raids on gang operations
Blackmail and coercion of corrupt officials
Evidence collection from police servers and criminal headquarters
Strategic property destruction to disrupt criminal operations

Public Perception:
Limited public awareness of their existence
Rumors of "powered vigilantes" in Dorchester
No official name in media reports
Some community support in neighborhoods affected by corruption

Relationships with Other Factions:
Direct opposition to Boston PD's corrupt elements
Conflict with local gangs, particularly those connected to Maria's death
