Recruitment Philosophy

Our recruitment process goes beyond traditional metrics. We seek individuals who demonstrate:

  • Exceptional metahuman or technological capabilities
  • Strong moral character and commitment to public service
  • Potential for significant personal and professional growth
  • Ability to work effectively in high-stress team environments
